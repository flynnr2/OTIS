# OTIS Non-Goals

OTIS is not initially intended to be:

- a consumer appliance;
- a turnkey plug-and-play network time server;
- a minimal-cost maker project;
- an all-purpose SDR framework;
- a generalized embedded framework;
- a replacement for laboratory-grade metrology equipment.

OTIS also does not initially attempt:
- picosecond-class TDC performance;
- advanced phase-noise characterization;
- universal hardware compatibility;
- arbitrary distributed synchronization.

These may become future research directions, but are intentionally outside the
initial MVP scope.
